Oscar Rodrigues, musician

I'm a musician from Porto, specializing in composition, workshop leading, and electronic music and programming

I currently work at Casa da Música, teach at Curso de Música Silva Monteiro and do some work as a freelance composer.

I'm interested in contemporary music, film, real time composition and the relationship between knowledge/science and art.

This website showcases some of the work I've done or are currently working on at the moment.
